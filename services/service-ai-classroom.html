Wolpaq's Smart AI Classroom solution transforms traditional learning spaces into dynamic, technology-driven environments that enhance teaching effectiveness and student engagement. Our integrated system brings together AI-powered interactive flat panels, high-definition audio-visual equipment, automated classroom controls, and a cloud-connected Learning Management System creating a seamless digital ecosystem built for the demands of modern education.

Deployed across schools and institutions, our Smart AI Classrooms support both in-person and hybrid learning models. Teachers gain powerful digital tools to deliver more engaging lessons, while administrators gain real-time visibility into classroom utilisation, device health and learning analytics through a centralised management dashboard.

Why Choose Smart AI Classrooms?

Conventional classrooms are no longer sufficient to prepare students for a technology-driven world. Our AI classroom solutions bridge the gap between traditional teaching and 21st-century learning demands while being simple enough for any teacher to use from day one.

Benefits of Smart AI Classrooms

Interactive Learning

AI-enabled flat panels make lessons visual, interactive and far more engaging for students of all ages.

Hybrid Ready

Seamlessly supports both in-person and online learners in the same classroom simultaneously.

Centralised LMS

Cloud-based Learning Management System for content delivery, assessments and progress tracking.

Smart Automation

Automated lighting, climate control and AV systems activated by occupancy saving energy too.

Easy for Teachers

Intuitive interfaces and comprehensive training ensure teachers can leverage the full system from day one.

Remote Management

Administrators manage all devices and content across every classroom from a single dashboard.

Planning & Deployment

Our team manages the entire deployment from needs assessment and classroom design through to hardware installation, software configuration and staff training.

  • AI flat panel and AV system installation and calibration
  • LMS setup, content migration and teacher onboarding
