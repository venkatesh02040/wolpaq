Wolpaq's Wind + Solar Hybrid Energy system is the cornerstone of our renewable energy offering. By combining rooftop solar photovoltaic panels with compact vertical axis wind turbines and a Li-Ion Battery Energy Storage System (BESS), we deliver a self-sustaining power ecosystem that generates clean electricity from two complementary natural sources ensuring reliable generation regardless of whether the sun is shining or the wind is blowing.

Our hybrid systems are engineered for institutions that demand uninterrupted power and meaningful reductions in grid dependence. Schools, hospitals and commercial buildings across India are already experiencing significant savings in electricity costs and a dramatically reduced carbon footprint thanks to this integrated approach to renewable energy generation.

Why Choose Our Hybrid System?

Unlike a standalone solar installation, a hybrid wind + solar system continues to generate power during overcast periods and after sunset making it the most resilient and energy-independent solution available for institutions that cannot afford downtime.

Benefits of Wind + Solar Hybrid Energy

24/7 Power Supply

Wind and solar generation complement each other to deliver round-the-clock clean electricity.

Reduced Energy Bills

Significantly lower grid electricity consumption translates directly into cost savings every month.

Battery Storage (BESS)

Integrated Li-Ion battery storage captures surplus energy for use during low-generation periods.

Zero Carbon Emissions

100% clean generation with no fuel costs and zero operational carbon emissions.

Real-Time Monitoring

Cloud-connected dashboards let you track energy generation and consumption live at any time.

25+ Year Lifespan

Industry-leading components with long operational lifespans and comprehensive warranties.

Frequently Asked Questions

A Wind + Solar Hybrid Energy system combines rooftop solar PV panels with vertical axis wind turbines and battery storage into a single integrated power ecosystem. By drawing from two renewable sources simultaneously, the system ensures continuous clean power generation even when one source is unavailable such as on cloudy days or calm nights.

Installation timelines vary based on system size and site conditions, but most institutional hybrid energy projects are completed within 4 to 8 weeks from site survey to commissioning. Our team handles all aspects of the installation including structural assessments, electrical work and grid interface configuration.

Hybrid energy systems are designed for minimal maintenance. Solar panels require periodic cleaning (typically quarterly) and annual performance checks. Wind turbines require annual mechanical inspections. Battery storage systems are monitored remotely 24/7 through our cloud platform. Wolpaq offers annual maintenance contracts to keep your system running at peak performance.

Depending on the existing electricity tariff and consumption pattern, most institutional hybrid energy systems achieve full payback within 4 to 7 years. With a system lifespan exceeding 25 years, institutions enjoy 18 to 20 years of virtually free electricity after payback representing exceptional long-term return on investment.
