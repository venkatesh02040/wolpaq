About Us

About Wolpaq Smart Solutions

Wolpaq is a Technology Infrastructure Company and an innovative organization focused on sustainable energy, smart automation, and future-ready infrastructure solutions. Our mission is to empower businesses and institutions through customized products and intelligent technologies for every sector.

With core focus on solar power systems, small wind turbines, smart classrooms, and advanced technology solutions, we position ourselves as a trusted one-stop destination for clean energy, operational efficiency, and smart infrastructure needs.

With expertise in customized hardware, integrated systems, and reliable support services, we deliver scalable solutions designed to enhance performance, sustainability, and long-term growth.

Meet Our Founder

10+

Years of Experience

Karthik Nandula

Co-Founder & CEO, Wolpaq

Karthik Nandula is a technology entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in IT infrastructure, surveillance systems, IoT-based building automation, and renewable energy solutions. His journey began in 2015 with a vision to deliver world-class technology solutions across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

From developing anti-animal accident systems in Kaziranga National Park to designing comprehensive infrastructure for DRDO, HAL, and BEL, Karthik has consistently pushed the boundaries of technology. His expertise spans CCTV surveillance, AI/ML analytics, smart building management, and Wind + Solar hybrid energy systems.

In 2024, he co-founded Wolpaq with his wife a holistic technology infrastructure company that serves as a single point of contact for schools, hotels, hospitals, and commercial buildings seeking smart, sustainable solutions.
