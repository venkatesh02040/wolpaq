2. Support Coverage Areas

Wolpaq provides technical support across all our service verticals:

  • Wind + Solar Hybrid Energy: Inverter faults, BESS issues, generation monitoring, panel performance, grid interface troubleshooting
  • Smart AI Classrooms: Interactive panel faults, AV system issues, LMS access, network connectivity, software and firmware updates
  • IT Infrastructure: Network downtime, Wi-Fi connectivity issues, switch/router faults, server performance, firewall and security support
  • CCTV & Surveillance: Camera offline events, NVR storage issues, remote access problems, video analytics configuration
  • Building Automation: BMS controller faults, sensor calibration, HVAC integration issues, lighting control failures, dashboard access

3. Response Time Commitments

The following response time targets apply to clients enrolled in a Wolpaq Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC). Clients without an AMC are served on a best-effort basis.

Priority Level Description Initial Response Resolution Target
Critical Complete system failure — energy, network or security system fully down Within 2 hours Same day / next business day
High Major component failure with significant operational impact Within 4 hours Within 2 business days
Medium Partial fault — system operational but degraded performance Within 8 hours Within 5 business days
Low Non-urgent query, configuration change, or general assistance Within 1 business day Within 10 business days

4. Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Wolpaq's AMC programme is designed to ensure the long-term health and performance of all installed systems. AMC subscribers receive:

  • Scheduled preventive maintenance visits (quarterly or bi-annual depending on system type)
  • Priority response and dedicated account management
  • 24/7 remote monitoring with proactive fault alerts
  • Software and firmware update management
  • Preferential pricing on spare parts, consumables, and system upgrades
  • Annual performance report for energy systems with generation and savings analysis

To enquire about AMC pricing and coverage for your installed system, please contact our team using the details below.

5. Raising a Support Request

When contacting our support team, please have the following information ready to help us respond quickly and accurately:

  • Your name, organisation, and site address
  • Wolpaq project reference number or installation date
  • A clear description of the fault or issue
  • Any error codes, alarm indicators, or monitoring dashboard readings observed
  • Photographs or video of the fault where safely possible
