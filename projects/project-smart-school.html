Project Overview

This project involved the complete design and installation of a Wind + Solar Hybrid Renewable Energy System for a school campus. The goal was to make the institution energy self-sufficient while dramatically reducing its electricity bills and carbon footprint.

Wolpaq designed an integrated system combining rooftop solar panels with vertical axis wind turbines and a Li-Ion Battery Energy Storage System (BESS). The system generates electricity from both solar and wind sources, ensuring power availability around the clock even during overcast days and at night.

Scope of Work

Our team handled end-to-end project execution from initial energy audit and feasibility study through to system design, procurement, installation, and commissioning. The project included real-time energy monitoring dashboards enabling the school management to track generation and consumption at all times.

  • Wind + Solar Hybrid energy system design and installation
  • Li-Ion Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) integration
  • Real-time energy monitoring and reporting dashboard
  • Self-sustaining outdoor lighting across the campus
